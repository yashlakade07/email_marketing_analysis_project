## 📌 Project Objective
To evaluate the effectiveness of email campaigns by analyzing engagement metrics and conversion rates, ultimately helping the marketing team improve ROI.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Tools:** Power BI, Excel
* **Analysis:** SQL, Python
* **Metrics Focus:** Engagement & Conversion Funnel

## 📊 Essential KPIs Analyzed
* **Open Rate:** Evaluated subject line effectiveness and sender reputation.
* **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Measured how compelling the content and calls-to-action were.
* **Conversion Rate:** Tracked the percentage of recipients who completed a purchase after clicking.
* **Bounce & Unsubscribe Rates:** Monitored list hygiene and subscriber fatigue.

## 📈 Strategic Recommendations
* **Segmented Targeting:** Recommended personalized subject lines for segments with low open rates.
* **CTA Optimization:** Identified specific layouts that led to a **higher CTR**, improving overall campaign performance.
